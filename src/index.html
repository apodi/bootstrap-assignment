Make music on day one

Most guitar lesson sites force beginners to do boring exercises and drills for months before actually making music. If you’re a beginner at Fret On! you will be making music on day 1, with our beginner Core Learning System.

Step-by-step system

Learning guitar is a lot easier when you have a step-by-step system to follow. Fret On! lessons are interconnected and organized to get slightly harder as you progress. You watch a video lesson, play along, and then click a “Next” button to go to the next lesson. Lessons have multiple camera angles, guitar tabs, jam tracks and everything else you need to learn..

you’re never alone

Our number one goal is to make you a better guitar player, and we will never give up on you! This means we have instructors, a guitar tech and a friendly staff that’s dedicated to helping you on the guitar journey.

ABOUT FRET ON

Picking up a guitar and making beautiful music is a great feeling and a big accomplishment. Sadly, most people believe they will never learn how to play. That’s why we started Fret On® – to provide online guitar lessons for those searching for an easy, fast, and fun way to learn guitar.
